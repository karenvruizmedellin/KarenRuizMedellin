About Me

face

Karen Vanessa Ruiz Medellín is a Colombian Psychologist with professional experience in the educational and clinical fields, particularly in teaching and supporting children, adolescents, and their families. She stands out for her creativity, empathy, adaptability, and ability to foster inclusion and holistic development.
